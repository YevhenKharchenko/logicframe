Features

  • Resonance Chains

    When you solve a puzzle, it doesn't just unlock the next area - it actually changes future puzzles, so your choices really matter.

    Tactile Crystal Mechanics

    The crystal pieces have this nice weight to them when you rotate them - we tried to make it feel like you're actually handling something physical.

  • A World Written in Light

    No long cutscenes here - the story unfolds as you solve puzzles, so if you pay attention, you'll pick up on the details.

    No Timers. No Penalties.

    Take your time with Aurenor - it's all about thinking through the puzzles, not racing against the clock.
