Features

  • Constraint-First Design

    No more of that mindless tile-shuffling stuff. Each puzzle is basically you building a logical argument from the ground up.

  • Cascade Solving

    Sometimes you'll place just one rule in exactly the right spot and suddenly half the puzzle solves itself. Kind of like watching dominoes fall, except it's your brain doing the work.

  • Contradiction Flash

    When you mess up (and you will), the grid basically calls you out and shows you exactly where things went wrong. Honestly, hunting down those contradictions becomes half the fun.

  • The Hidden Gallery

    Every solved frame reveals original artwork: illustrations, photographs, and patterns commissioned for the game.
