How It Works

  • Read the Grid

    Each puzzle gives you a blank grid with a hidden picture inside. All you have to work with are these logic rules.

  • Place Your Rules

    You'll drag these constraint rules - stuff like comparisons, exclusions, that kind of thing - right onto the edges of cells. Get it in the right place and suddenly other parts just... figure themselves out.

  • Reveal the Frame

    Once everything clicks - and man, when it does - this whole picture just shows up out of nowhere. Full color, beautiful artwork. It's honestly magical seeing your logic turn into something visual.
